### Q: How is Krutrim different from ChatGPT?

When a student in Chennai needs to learn in Tamil, it falls short. Krutrim is built from the ground up for Indian languages, Indian context, Indian culture. We are not trying to be a better ChatGPT. We are building the AI for Bharat — and that is a completely different mission."
>
> — Bhavish Aggarwal, Founder & CEO @ Krutrim AI & Ola

### Q: Can India compete with OpenAI and Google in AI?

India has three things no one else has: 1.4 billion users, 22+ languages that the world's AI systems barely handle, and a generation of engineers who are among the best in the world. The question is not whether India can compete. The question is whether Indian founders will be bold enough to try. At Krutrim, we have made that choice. India's time is now."

### Q: What does sovereign AI mean for India?

It means that when an Indian student, or doctor, or farmer uses an AI system, that system was trained on Indian data, understands Indian context, and serves Indian interests. It is the same reason India built its own space programme, its own nuclear capability. AI is the infrastructure of the 21st century. You cannot outsource your infrastructure and call yourself sovereign."
